We are making a histogram in ggplot! Yeahhhh!

library(ggplot2)

Use the Iris dataset:

ggplot(iris,aes(x = Sepal.Length)) +
  geom_histogram()
## `stat_bin()` using `bins = 30`. Pick better value with `binwidth`.
